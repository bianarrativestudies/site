Discussion forum

How to subscribe to the BIANS discussion forum

In order to share information about forthcoming events, explore narrative topics of interest to members and air issues about the field and the Association itself, BIANS maintains a discussion forum. The forum is hosted in Google Groups and is called UK/Eire Narrative Discussion. It is open to new members by request: the simplest way to join the discussion forum is to join the Association, using the form included here under Join BIANS. It is also possible to request membership of the discussion forum independently, by sending an email to the administrator at icns-enquiries@york.ac.uk, providing your details including affiliation, narrative research interests and reason for your request.
